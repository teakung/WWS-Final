Start Guide

-Lie down on the floor face down with your feet close together.

-Place your hands shoulder width apart.

-While maintaining a straight body, lower yourself down until your chest touches the ground.

-Push yourself up
-lie faceup on the ground

-bend your knees to let your heels go parallel the floor

-place your hands together behind your head

-flex and pull your upper torso up until your elbow can touch your knees before slowly lower yourself back to your original position

-Select your lap count

-Place your device where you can press the button when you run back to it

-run and come to press the button for each passing lap

-Place the device for it to be easily reachable.

-Run as fast as you can. and touch the device each time you make a lap past it

-stand straight feets together hands by your sides

-jump and place your feet away from the other and place your hand together over your head

-jump again and get back to your original position
